Applications

Who Benefits from VoxEasy?

Our platform serves diverse patient populations across multiple care settings

Intubated ICU Patients

15M+ patients are intubated annually in the U.S. VoxEasy gives them a voice during ventilation, reducing anxiety and improving nurse response times by allowing direct communication of pain, comfort needs, and concerns.

Post-Operative Recovery

Patients recovering from throat surgery, tracheostomy, or head/neck procedures can communicate immediately post-op without strain. Faster pain assessment and better comfort lead to improved recovery outcomes.

Stroke & ALS Patients

Neurological conditions often affect speech. VoxEasy provides a reliable way for patients with aphasia, ALS, or stroke-related speech loss to maintain independence and communicate with caregivers and loved ones.

Hospice & Palliative Care

End-of-life patients deserve dignity. VoxEasy ensures they can express wishes, share feelings, and maintain connection with family even when voice is fading. Preserving humanity in the most vulnerable moments.
